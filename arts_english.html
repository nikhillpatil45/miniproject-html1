Arts - English Course

The English course in the Arts department provides an in-depth study of the English language, literature, and its cultural impact. Students will explore various forms of literary expression, enhance their critical thinking and analytical skills, and develop a deeper understanding of language and its use in different contexts.

Course Content Highlights:

  • Study of Classic and Modern Literature
  • Grammar and Linguistics
  • Creative Writing
  • Literary Analysis
  • Language in Society

Teachers:

  • Professor James Anderson
  • Ms. Evelyn Ramirez
